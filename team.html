This is a team feature. It supports team members to collaborate, mine faster, and share profits together. Method of profit sharing : ⦿ Team leader will receive 10% of the total profit. ⦿ Remaining 90% will be evenly distributed among the participating members based on their POW. What does POW mean ? POW is considered a measure of contribution to help the team mine faster. For example : The team's profit is $1000, and 100 POW has been contributed to the team. ⦿ 10% will go to the leader ($100). ⦿ 90% will be distributed among the remaining members ($900). ⇒ If you hold 2 POW, you will receive $18
